Cell Studio

Cell Studio is a blockchain software development company with a dedicated focus on the BTC ecosystem, committed to advancing the Nervos BTCKB initiative. Our goal is to facilitate the integration of BTC and CKB, empowering CKB to emerge as the premier Bitcoin Layer2 solution fully interoperable with BTC, utilizing a "PoW+UTXO" framework.

Much like ConsenSys' pivotal role in the Ethereum ecosystem, Cell Studio is poised to play a crucial part within the Nervos ecosystem. Founded under the auspices of the Nervos ecosystem fund InNervation and helmed by Nervos co-founder Cipher, our mission is to propel the development and prosperity of the Nervos ecosystem. We achieve this by harnessing potent smart contract capabilities to enable a diverse array of assets on the BTC Layer1.
